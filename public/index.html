About us

We started in 2024 as a small queer 5K running crew in Abbey Wood, just wanting somewhere safe and friendly to lace up together. Two years on, we're a free LGBT+ club that runs, climbs and hikes across South East London — most of us live in Abbey Wood, Thamesmead and Woolwich, but everyone from SE London is welcome ☺️

There's no pace test, no minimum distance, and no pressure to be the fastest or the fittest in the room. If you're LGBT+ or an ally and you fancy moving your body in good company, you're already one of us.

What we do

🏃

Running

Where it all began. Friendly 5K-friendly group runs around Abbey Wood and the Thames Path, plus a Saturday Parkrun crew in Woolwich.

🧗

Climbing

Weekly bouldering and rope sessions at The Reach in Woolwich. Total beginners welcome — there'll always be someone to show you the ropes.

🥾

Hiking

Occasional day trips out of London into the Kent and Surrey countryside — a chance to swap pavements for woodland, hills and a proper pub lunch.
