About us

We started in 2024 as a small queer running crew in Abbey Wood, just wanting somewhere safe and friendly to lace up together. Two years on, we've grown into a community that runs, hikes and climbs across South East London — and we'd love to have you along.

There's no pace test, no minimum distance, and no pressure to be the fastest or the fittest in the room. If you're LGBT+ or an ally and you fancy moving your body in good company, you're already one of us.

What we do

🏃

Running

Where it all began. Friendly group runs from easy social paces to faster sessions, with routes around Abbey Wood, Thamesmead and the Thames Path.

🥾

Hiking

Day trips out of London into the Kent and Surrey countryside — a chance to swap pavements for woodland, hills and a proper pub lunch.

🧗

Climbing

Indoor bouldering and rope sessions at South East London walls. Total beginners welcome — there'll always be someone to show you the ropes.

Where we meet

Our home turf is Abbey Wood, Thamesmead and Woolwich, with members joining from across South East London and beyond. Specific meeting points and times shift week to week — the easiest way to stay in the loop is on Strava and Instagram.

Come join us

Two ways in — pick whichever feels easiest. We'll see you soon.
